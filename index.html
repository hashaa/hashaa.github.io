Parameter Space:$\Theta\subset\mathbb{R}^d$ Extremum Estimator: $\{\hat{\theta}_n;n\geq1\}$ \begin{equation} \hat{\theta}_n\in\Theta \quad\mbox{and}\quad Q_n(\hat{\theta}_n)\leq \inf_{\theta\in\Theta}Q_n(\theta)+o_p(1) \end{equation} where $Q_n(\cdot)$ is objective function. $\hat{\theta}_n$ approximately minimizes the objective function.
